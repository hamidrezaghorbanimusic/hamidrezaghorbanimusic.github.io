Biography

Hamidreza Ghorbani is an Iranian singer, songwriter, composer, multi-instrumentalist, and independent music producer whose artistic vision blends Pop Rock, Orchestral Pop, Fusion, and World Music into a distinctive and emotionally expressive sound.

Since beginning his musical journey in 2006, he has continuously developed his craft through songwriting, composition, performance, arranging, and music production. His music is characterized by cinematic arrangements, meaningful lyrics, and a deep emotional connection that resonates with audiences across cultures.

Between 2021 and 2022, Hamidreza collaborated on several international album projects with renowned musicians from Europe and the United States, including legendary bassist Peter Baltes, together with artists such as Sarah Rebecca (Sarah Gannon) and Shay Watson. These collaborations expanded his artistic perspective and introduced his music to a wider international audience.

His discography includes the internationally released albums Songs of Hope and Peace (2021), Love Is Enough (2021), Life Goes On (2022), and the Persian-language album Rooz o Shab (Day And Night), released in 2026.

As a multi-instrumentalist, Hamidreza performs classical guitar, electric guitar, bass guitar, piano, harmonica, and synthesizer. He is actively involved in every stage of the creative process—from songwriting and composition to arranging, music production, artistic direction, visual design, mixing, mastering, and digital publishing—ensuring that every release reflects his artistic vision and commitment to excellence.

"For Hamidreza Ghorbani, music is more than sound—it is a universal language that connects people beyond borders, cultures, and generations."
