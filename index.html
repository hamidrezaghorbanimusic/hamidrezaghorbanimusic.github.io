About Hamidreza

Hamidreza Ghorbani is an Iranian Singer-Songwriter, lyricist, composer, Multi-instrumentalist, Sound Engineer and Music Producer born on July 30, 1991, in Isfahan, Iran.

He is known for international collaborations with American and European musicians through several albums and single tracks.

Artist Information

Music Genres: Pop Rock • Fusion • Orchestral

Instruments: Classical Guitar, Electric Guitar, Bass Guitar, Piano, Harmonica, Synthesizer

Active Years: 2006 – Present

Collaborations: Peter Baltes, Sarah Rebecca (Gannon), Shay Watson
